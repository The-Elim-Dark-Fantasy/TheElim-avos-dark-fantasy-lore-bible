Book Introduction

Step into the Grimdark world of The Darkness Steals The Light — An epic dark fantasy series of murderous plots, resurrected gods, war, magic and betrayal. The world of Avos is doomed, and only Lord Varesh can alter its fate.

The Darkness Steals The Light

Agreat darkness rises—Ana , a dragon of unparalleled brutality, the ancient god reborn. Ana’s wicked taint spreads like a plague, eroding the lines between good and evil, festering until it corrupts all life. The loyal servants of Ana plot in the shadows, spinning treacherous webs of deceit, betrayal, and bloody violence.

The world of Avos is doomed, and only one man can alter its fate: Lord Varesh, the last surviving Elim magi. He embarks on a perilous quest to rekindle the fading light in the decaying world. With him stand Jain, a charming mercenary and master swordsman haunted by his past; Danan, a humble scribe with a hidden past; and the lethal assassin Nubina.

Fear and greed reign supreme as malicious plots unfold in a trail of bloody corpses. The stability of the empire crumbles to ash as ambitious kings play their devious games. Heroes unite, and ancient foes rise as the fate of Avos hangs in the balance. No one is safe as good and evil collide. Will light prevail, or will Avos be consumed by shadow, blood, and fire?

The Darkness Steals The Light — Book one of the epic grimdark fantasy adventure series, The Elim Chronicles.
